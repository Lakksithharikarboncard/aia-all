Problem

The AIA Dashboard has two tabs. Overview shows one set of numbers. Receivables and Payables sit together in another tab. Charts and data are everywhere, but nowhere does it tell you what it all means. You have to jump between tabs and piece things together in your head to figure out what is wrong, what is unusual, and what needs attention first. That takes effort. And it makes the tool feel slow.

The problem: the data is there, but its meaning is not.

Proposed Solution

Launch AI Insights (BETA) as a compact advisory module on the Dashboard Overview tab. It reads data from charts already on the dashboard and surfaces the most important signals.

How insights work:

  • Every insight is backed by real numbers from your data, not guesses
  • Insights are ordered by how serious, how big, and how recent the signal is
  • No generic advice. Every insight is specific to your business
  • Each insight shows what area it came from (Overview or Receivables+Payables)
  • All content is checked against fixed rules before it shows up on screen

Objectives

AI Insights helps business owners and finance professionals answer three questions in under 30 seconds:

  1. What is the most important signal in my numbers right now?
  2. Why does it matter to the business?
  3. Which metric or chart should I look at next?

Feature Requirements

Placement and Behaviour

  • AI Insights lives only on the Dashboard Overview tab, below the top KPI cards and above the secondary charts.
  • Three insights show by default. Users can click "Show more" to see up to five.
  • Uses a compact accordion. All rows start collapsed. Only one insight can be open at a time.

Feedback on Insights

  • Each expanded card shows "Helpful" and "Not helpful" buttons.
  • If a user clicks "Not helpful", they can pick a reason: too generic, not accurate, already knew this, or hard to understand.
  • Feedback improves detection rules, copy quality, and helps track module usefulness over time.

AI Capabilities

What the AI does

  • Reads the complete raw financial data (not just pre-computed charts or aggregates) and identifies meaningful signals
  • Generates plain English insight cards with exact numbers, comparisons, and time periods
  • Writes in simple business language, avoiding accounting jargon like DSO, DPO, or AR
  • Uses soft language ("may indicate", "suggests", "is worth reviewing") instead of hard claims
  • Outputs a structured JSON object that the system can validate before displaying

What the AI does not do

  • It does not decide severity or materiality thresholds (those are rule based)
  • It does not invent numbers, causes, benchmarks, or predictions
  • It does not trigger any automated actions or workflows
  • It does not replace professional accounting judgment

Principle

The AI reads the raw data and surfaces what matters. Fixed rules validate the output before anyone sees it. Charts on the dashboard show aggregated views of the same raw data.

TL;DR

AI Insights is a compact module on the Dashboard Overview tab. It reads raw financial data, picks out what is important, and writes 3 to 5 plain English cards explaining what changed, why it matters, and what to look at next. Cards show severity (Critical, Watch, Positive), cite exact numbers, and include feedback buttons. The system validates all output before display. No generic advice, no automation, no predictions. Ships as BETA on the Overview tab only.
