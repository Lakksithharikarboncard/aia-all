AI Accountant Billing & Access PRD

AI Accountant · Billing and Access Control Module

Status: Draft Updated: 13 May 2026

Problem Statement

AIA automates financial document processing for Indian SMBs: Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Bank Statements, GST Reconciliation, Reporting, and Tally / Zoho integration.

Every customer today goes through a sales conversation before they can use the product. That's intentional. What's not intentional is everything that happens after the deal closes.

There is no billing module.

  • Customers don't know what plan they're on.
  • Customers don't know what they've paid for or when it renews.
  • The team has no single place to activate a customer, assign modules, or track payment.
  • Access is enabled manually, after someone confirms payment over chat.
  • Renewals are followed up by hand.

The product works. The commercial layer around it doesn't exist yet.

High-Level Approach & Goals

This is not a self-serve billing system. AIA is sold through demos and that stays the same.

What we're building is a control layer, a thin module that connects a closed deal to an active, paying customer without manual coordination.

The split is simple:

  • Billing software handles money: checkout, payments, subscriptions, invoices, renewals
  • AIA handles access: which customer is active, which modules they can use

Goals

Audit everything Every status, plan, and module change is logged with actor and reason
No drift between AIA and billing software Automatic updates from the billing software keep account status and module access in sync

What this is not

No self-serve signup. No public pricing page. No free trial without BD approval. No token tracking. No usage-based billing. Those come later.

Solution & Key Features

One module. Two surfaces: an admin side for BD/Growth and CS, and a customer side for the end user.

BD owns the demo and first payment. Once payment lands, the account hands off to CS for ongoing management: renewals, exceptions, plan changes, and everything else.

User Stories

BD / Growth

As BD, I want a quick way to anchor a price during a demo so I can close the deal with confidence.

  • Opens Pricing Calculator
  • Selects required modules, enters expected volumes, picks billing frequency
  • System suggests a tier and price
  • BD uses the number to anchor the commercial conversation
  • Nothing is saved, reference only

As BD, I want one place to activate a customer so I do not coordinate across chat threads.

  • Opens Create Customer
  • Fills the intake form; Live Pricing Preview shows the recommended plan
  • Can override with a custom plan, which syncs to the billing software automatically
  • Chooses Send payment link or 14-day trial
  • On submit: billing customer created, checkout or signup invite generated, audit log written
  • Sends the link to the customer with a single click (email or copy-paste into any channel)

Customer Success

As CS, I want to see what needs my attention without scrolling so I can pick the next account to work on.

  • Opens Overview
  • Sees a single attention strip with Trials Ending, Renewals Due, Payment Due, Grace, Frozen
  • Clicks any segment: Customers list filtered to that status

As CS, I want to see and change everything about one customer in one screen.

  • Opens Customer Detail
  • Overview tab: company, primary user, billing contact, internal notes, checkout/signup link, Send Email action
  • Plan & Modules tab: assign or change plan; see unlocked vs locked modules
  • Payments tab: billing details, renewal date, checkout link, refresh, open billing portal
  • Admin Actions tab: freeze, unfreeze, change status, update modules, extend trial, convert to paid. Every action requires a reason.

As CS, I want to maintain the plan catalogue so it stays small and current.

  • Opens Packages
  • Adds, edits, activates, deactivates, or deletes plans
  • Delete is blocked when customers are still on that plan

As CS, I want a tamper-evident record of every change.

  • Opens Audit Log
  • Filters by actor or action
  • Sees old to new value, reason, timestamp

End-user (AI Accountant Customer)

As a customer, I want to know what I am paying for, when it renews, and what I can use.

  • Opens the product, navigates to Billing & Plan
  • Sees current plan, price, billing frequency, renewal date
  • Sees included modules and locked modules
  • Opens billing customer portal via Manage Billing

As a customer, I want to ask for access to a module I do not have without emailing.

  • Clicks Request Access on a locked module
  • Adds a short message
  • Submits. CS reviews and approves or rejects.

As a customer, I want clear instructions when something is wrong with my account.

  • If payment pending: full-page gate with Complete Payment
  • If trial expired: dashboard visible, modules locked, directed to contact the growth team
  • If grace: warning banner with grace end date; access retained; pre/post-billing alerts shown
  • If frozen: full-page gate with Manage Billing and Contact CS

Automated (no one triggers this)

The billing software sends updates to AIA whenever something changes, keeping everything in sync without manual work.

  • Every incoming update is verified to prevent tampering
  • Updates are never processed twice
  • Trial starts: account set to trial, dates recorded
  • Payment confirmed: account set to active, renewal date set
  • Renewal paid: renewal date bumped forward
  • Subscription cancelled: account set to inactive
  • Subscription revoked: account set to frozen
  • Payment received after grace: account set back to active
  • Customer details updated: module access synced to match what was paid for
  • Grace period: 1 week. If no payment by the end, account auto‑freezes.
  • Pre and post‑billing alerts shown inside the product.

What Happens When

What happens when a customer cancels during trial?

The billing software notifies AIA. The account moves to inactive. The customer can no longer access the product.

Can a frozen or inactive customer come back?

Yes. If the customer pays or CS manually reactivates the account, it moves back to active. The customer goes through the normal payment flow.

What happens when a plan is changed mid-cycle?

CS assigns a new plan from the Customer Detail screen. The billing software is updated. The change takes effect immediately. If the new plan has fewer modules, the customer loses access to the removed modules. If it has more, the new modules unlock right away.

How does BD know the first payment landed?

The billing software updates AIA automatically. The customer moves from payment pending to active. BD can see this in the customer list. No manual check needed.

What exactly is logged in the audit trail?

Every change to a customer, plan, or module assignment is recorded. Each entry shows who made the change, what changed (old value and new value), why (the reason typed in), and when (timestamp).

Do BD and CS have different permissions?

Both roles have full access to the admin side. The difference is who does what: BD handles demos and first payment. CS handles everything after. Both can see and do everything in the admin portal.

How does the customer access the billing portal?

Inside the AI Accountant product, there is a Billing & Plan menu item. Clicking it opens the white‑label billing app. The customer does not need a separate URL or login.
